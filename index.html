About The Festival

The Brac Presents 1st-SN Bose National Science Festival-2025 is a celebration of scientific innovation, critical thinking, and collaborative learning. Named after the legendary physicist Satyendra Nath Bose, this festival aims to inspire the next generation of scientists and innovators.

Organized by Biggan Adda, Sponsored by Brac, this inaugural event brings together over 20 educational institutions and more than 800 participants in a series of competitive and collaborative events designed to showcase scientific talent and creativity.

From challenging Olympiads to cutting-edge IT Hackathons, from eloquent Public Speaking to brain-teasing Quizzes, the festival offers a platform for participants to demonstrate their knowledge, skills, and passion for science.

Race against time to solve the classic Rubik's Cube puzzle faster than your competitors in this exciting open category event.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the festival?

The festival is open to students from all educational institutions across Bangladesh. Different events have specific category requirements (Secondary, Higher Secondary, and Senior/Undergraduate). Some events like Chess and Rubik's Cube competitions are open to all categories.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a nominal registration fee that varies depending on the event. The exact fee structure will be available on the registration form. The fee helps cover administrative costs, certificates, and prizes.

How do I prepare for the National Science Olympiad?

Preparation involves studying the syllabus content of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and ICT for your respective category. Focus on fundamental concepts and their applications. Practice with previous years' question papers and standard Olympiad preparation books available in the market.

What should I bring to the onsite competitions?

Participants should bring their registration confirmation, valid ID card, necessary stationery items, and any specific materials required for their events. For events like the Rubik's Cube contest, participants must bring their own Rubik's Cube. For the IT Hackathon finals, teams should bring their own laptops.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, participants can register for multiple events as long as the event schedules don't overlap. However, we recommend focusing on events that align with your strengths to maximize your chances of success. Check the detailed schedule on the registration form to plan accordingly.

How can I contact the organizers?

You can contact the organizing committee via email at bigganadda2019@gmail.com. For urgent matters, you can also reach out through our social media channels linked in the footer section.
