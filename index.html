About The Festival

The Brac Presents 1st-SN Bose National Science Festival-2025 is a celebration of scientific innovation, critical thinking, and collaborative learning. Named after the legendary physicist Satyendra Nath Bose, this festival aims to inspire the next generation of scientists and innovators.

Organized by Biggan Adda, Sponsored by Brac, this inaugural event brings together over 20 educational institutions and more than 800 participants in a series of competitive and collaborative events designed to showcase scientific talent and creativity.

From challenging Olympiads to cutting-edge IT Hackathons, from eloquent Public Speaking to brain-teasing Quizzes, the festival offers a platform for participants to demonstrate their knowledge, skills, and passion for science.

When & Where

Curzon Hall,University of Dhaka, May 23-25 (Friday to Sunday)

Who Can Participate

Open to students from all educational institutions. Teams and individual participants are welcome.

Innovation & Display

Event Details

  • Contestants of Higher Secondary & Senior categories can participate in this segment.
  • Must participate as a team with a maximum of 3 participants.
  • Projects must focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, Innovation, New Business Ideas, or similar areas.
  • Can be working models, prototypes, software, or conceptual models.
  • Must address real-world problems or offer novel solutions.
  • Each team gets 5–10 minutes to present and demonstrate their project to judges, followed by 2–5 minutes of Q&A.
  • Registration Fee: FREE (Biggan Adda has made this event Registration fee free for all categories participants)
Judging Criteria
  • Originality & Innovation (20%)
  • Scientific Thought (20%)
  • Practical Application (20%)
  • Presentation & Clarity (15%)
  • Teamwork & Communication (15%)
  • Sustainability or Social Impact (10%)

Projects must be original; copied work will be disqualified.

Rubik's Cube Contest (Open)

Event Details

  • Categories:
    • Standard Speed Solving
    • Blindfolded Solving
  • Open to all ages and skill levels
  • Participants must bring their own cubes (non-programmable)
  • Registration Fee: ৳150 (includes cash-out charges)

Competition Rules & Format

  • Each participant gets 5 attempts
  • The average of those 5 times will be used for scoring (best and worst will be deducted and the average of the remaining three solves will be counted)
  • For Blindfolded solving, Best of three system will be followed
  • 15 seconds of inspection time will be given before each solve
  • 2 or more rounds may be held depending on the number of participants
  • WCA-style penalties will apply
  • Cube breaking during solve will result in disqualification
  • Final decisions rest with the event delegates and organizing team
  • WCA rules will be strictly followed
